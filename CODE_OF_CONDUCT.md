# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project respectful and harassment-free.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind and constructive.
- Assume good intent.
- Keep criticism focused on the work.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Deliberate disruption of discussion or development.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or block participants who violate this code.
